The Evolution keywords

NameDescription
GrowthUpon reaching 2 Growth gain 1 Energy and reduce Growth by 2
VigorIncreases the damage of your next Attack.
DroneDrones are 0 cost attacks which benefit from Potency, are Ethereal and Exhaust when played.
AdaptConsume a channeled Gene. Add its effect to the card until the end of the combat and shuffle this card in your draw pile. Adapted effects target the same enemy as the card if possible.
FormTrigger the following effect if you have a Gene of this type, otherwise Channel one.
Fate XPut random cards from your draw pile matching X on top of your draw pile, then Scry them.
LustReduce the base damage of the next attack by this amount.
RageWhenever you play an Attack this turn, gain this amount of Block.
RandomizedRandomized Forms are selected when the card is created and vary from a card to another.
ShadowsWhen Shadows reaches its threshold it deals 2 damage per stack and applies 1 Weak to all enemies. The first threshold is 1.
ShiftRecreate the gene at the back of the orb line.
